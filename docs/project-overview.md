TON Malam AI - Project Overview

Overview

TON Malam AI is an AI-powered educational platform built to help Hausa-speaking users learn TON, STON.fi, DeFi, and blockchain technology through localized content, interactive lessons, quizzes, and AI assistance.

The platform focuses on reducing language barriers that prevent millions of African users from participating in the TON ecosystem.

---

Problem Statement

Most blockchain and DeFi educational resources are available only in English.

This creates challenges for many Hausa-speaking users who want to learn:

- TON Blockchain
- STON.fi
- Wallet Security
- Token Swaps
- Staking
- Yield Farming

As a result, many users struggle to onboard safely into Web3.

---

Solution

TON Malam AI provides:

- AI-powered Hausa Tutor
- Beginner-friendly lessons
- Interactive quizzes
- Security awareness training
- STON.fi educational resources

All content is designed to be simple, practical, and accessible.

---

Target Audience

- Hausa-speaking crypto beginners
- TON newcomers
- African Web3 communities
- Telegram users

---

Core Features

AI Tutor

Users can ask questions in Hausa and receive educational responses.

Learning Academy

Structured lessons covering TON and STON.fi fundamentals.

Quiz Center

Interactive quizzes to reinforce learning.

Security Center

Education on avoiding scams and protecting wallets.

Progress Tracking

Monitor learning achievements and completed lessons.

---

Hackathon Goal

Build a functional MVP that demonstrates how localized AI education can accelerate adoption of TON and STON.fi across Africa.

---

Long-Term Vision

Become the leading Hausa-language onboarding and education platform for TON users across Africa.
